MONKEY

Monkeys are primates known for their intelligence, agility, and playful behavior. They live in trees and on the ground in various habitats around the world.

Monkeys are social animals and often live in groups called troops.

Fun fact: Some monkeys use tools, such as sticks, to extract insects from tree bark or to crack open nuts. Many species have prehensile tails that act like a fifth limb!

Conservation note: Many monkey species are threatened by habitat loss and hunting. Zoos and sanctuaries help protect endangered monkeys and educate the public about their importance.
