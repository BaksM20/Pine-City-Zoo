PANDA

The giant panda is a bear native to south central China. It is easily recognized by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body.

Pandas are known for their love of bamboo, which makes up most of their diet.

Fun fact: Despite their bulk, pandas are excellent climbers and can swim! A newborn panda is about the size of a stick of butter and is born pink and blind.

Conservation note: Giant pandas were once endangered, but thanks to conservation efforts, their status has improved. Continued habitat protection is vital for their future.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

The pandas are located in the bamboo forest section of the zoo.
