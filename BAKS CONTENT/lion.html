LION

The lion is one of the largest cats in the world and is known as the king of the jungle. Lions live in groups called prides and are found in Africa and India.

They are known for their majestic manes and powerful roars.

Fun fact: A lion’s roar can be heard up to 8 kilometers away! Female lions do most of the hunting, while males protect the pride’s territory.

Conservation note: Lion populations are declining due to habitat loss and conflict with humans. Conservationists are working to protect wild lions and their habitats.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

The lions are located in the savannah section of the zoo.
