GORILLA

Gorillas are the largest primates and are native to the forests of central Sub-Saharan Africa. They are known for their strength, intelligence, and gentle nature.

Gorillas live in groups called troops and are led by a dominant silverback male.

Fun fact: Gorillas use a variety of vocalizations, gestures, and facial expressions to communicate. They have been observed using tools in the wild, such as sticks to gauge water depth.

Conservation note: Gorillas are critically endangered due to habitat loss, disease, and poaching. Conservation programs focus on protecting forests and supporting local communities.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

The gorillas are located in the rainforest section of the zoo.
